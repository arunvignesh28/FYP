There lived a hare and a tortoise in a jungle. The hare kept bragging about its speed and all the animals were tired of it.
Tired of the bragging of the speedy hare, the tortoise challenges it to a race.All the animals in the jungle were excited and  ready to watch the race.The hare is overconfident about its speed and accepts the competition.The competition begins and the hare runs as fast as it can. Soon it gets tired. The hare thinks it is way ahead and decides to sleep,Thinking that there’s plenty of time to relax before tortoise can catch up with it. 
Meanwhile, the tortoise continues to walk slowly, until it reaches the finish line. The overslept hare wakes up  and finds that the tortoise is way ahead of it in the race.
The tortoise beats the hare as it continued without giving up.
The tortoise won the race.
